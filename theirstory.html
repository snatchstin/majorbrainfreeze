It seems like just yesterday Pete and Leree were happily running their restaurant businesses. Life seemed good but they were a tad  frustrated with the fact that they didn't have a dessert option.  A significant mentor of theirs, asked that important question, "Why not start your own yogurt business? You guys would do great !"  Four months later Freddie Freeze was born and "Major Brain Freeze Yogurt" was open to the public.

 

Right fron the get go, it was a huge hit.  People flocked from all over Southern California to experience making their own yogurt with a universe of different toppings.  They found that using only the best natural flavored yogurts, and ONLY fresh fruit (cut daily), would attract people, like bees to honey.  They have had so much fun with this, that they have opened up 4 different locations.  You have to come try out this place.  It is sooo cool.  Stop by, and create your own unique yogurt, and just kick back in one of their lazy bean bag chairs, and just relax and enjoy.

 

Sometimes in life people need a new idea or two and a little encouragement.   Always try to think outside your box and entertain ideas that help you stay out of your comfort zone.  For Pete and Leree, "Major Brain Freeze Yogurt" has been an example of this.  All because of a mentors suggestion and encouragement....Thanks Tom !
